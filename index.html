Your Data Is An Asset

Your AI interactions are worth real money.

AI companies pay billions for training data. Right now, they scrape the open web and call it a day. But real, high-quality family conversations — the kind that actually make AI smarter — are the most valuable data on the planet.

With FamCloud, every interaction your family has makes the AI better — and it all stays on your device. Someday, when the demand is right, you'll be able to opt in and sell your household's anonymized data to AI companies. You own it. You get paid.
